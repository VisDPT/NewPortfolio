All About Dr. V


I am a Junior Full-Stack Web Developer who just can’t get enough of Front End Web Development! My coding obsessions are HTML, CSS, Wordpress, Javascript, & jQuery, but I am always up for the challenge of learning a brand-new language!


So you may be wondering "How does a Doctor of Physical Therapy wind up in web development?” As a physical therapist who moved around and lived in 5 cities over 6 years, I started a Wordpress blog to share all my tips for a smooth and successful move. I quickly discovered my passion for front end web development as I would spend more time designing in Wordpress than actually writing blog entries. That led me to pursue my passion and I successfully completed the Full Stack Web & Application Development Certificate Program at the University of Texas-Austin.
